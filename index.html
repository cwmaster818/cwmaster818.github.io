FAQ

Common client questions

Do you replace a licensed lawyer, auditor or tax advisor?

No. CW Consulting provides payroll, HR and administrative compliance support. Legal, audit, tax-planning or regulated advisory matters require the appropriate licensed professional.

Can you support cross-border employees?

Yes. Support can cover payroll documentation, G permit context, social insurance coordination and source-tax review for Switzerland–Italy–France situations.

How should a client prepare before booking?

Prepare company canton or country, number of employees, permit situation, payroll issue, source-tax question and any relevant correspondence.
